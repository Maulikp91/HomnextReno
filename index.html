About Us

Unique Solutions for Residential and Commercial Spaces!

Homnext Inc is your trusted partner for professional renovation services, offering expert solutions for legal basements, home extensions, kitchen and bathroom remodels, and full residential or commercial upgrades. We also specialize in transforming your home with custom wall paneling, stylish TV wall designs, and modern fireplace installations. Our skilled team ensures every project meets building codes while delivering modern, functional designs tailored to your needs.

We pride ourselves on transparency, quality craftsmanship, and building lasting client relationships. Whether you're upgrading a single room, adding a new feature like a fireplace or custom wall paneling, or planning a complete renovation, we work closely with you to bring your vision to life—on time, within budget, and with results you can trust.
